Description:

The events take place in the 1930s and tell about the misadventures of Joseph Joestar, the grandson of Jonathan Joestar. Joseph will have to visit different parts of the world, make new friends, learn the secret of the origin of the stone mask, meet the Nazis, hone his skills in mastering supernatural abilities, and, finally, fight with the recently awakened unrivaled warriors of antiquity who are eager to bring the whole world to its knees and rise above the very essence of life.

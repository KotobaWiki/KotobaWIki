Description:

1988. Kujo Jotaro, the grandson of Joseph Joestar, discovers supernatural powers in himself while participating in another school brawl, and not knowing how to deal with it, locks himself in a local prison. After some time, his mother, unable to convince her son to leave the cell, asks his father, Jotaro's grandfather, to come to his place, who will explain to his grandson what kind of mysterious power is hidden in him and what its purpose is.

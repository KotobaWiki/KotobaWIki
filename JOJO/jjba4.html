Description:

In the provincial Japanese town of Morioh, no big events have happened until fate brings a bow and arrow there, capable of awakening a stand in a person - a physical manifestation of his life energy, will and fighting spirit. Amazing events begin to happen one after another, and against their background, reports of the disappearances of young girls are completely lost. But the mysterious maniac does not have much time left to walk free, because the company of Higashikata Josuke, the illegitimate son of Joseph Joestar, indestructible as a diamond, stands guard over the city.

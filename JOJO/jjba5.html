Description:

This time the action moves to Italy, and the main character is Giorno Giovanna - a young but very ambitious guy, who is no more no less than the son of the infamous Dio Brando. However, unlike his father, Giorno chose a completely different path and other means to achieve his cherished dream. He wants to take the place of the godfather, the head of the Italian mafia, and establish control over lawlessness and crime. But the main snag is that all the key members of this organization are users of stands. On his difficult path to the top of the criminal world, Giorno will find loyal comrades, face a large number of opponents, learn many secrets and solve many riddles.

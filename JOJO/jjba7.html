Description:

The year is 1890. Entrepreneur Stephen Steele is organizing the largest horse race of all time, across America. $50,000,000 is at stake. Riders from all over the world are gathering to compete for the prize. It is a truly unique race, and its name is Steel Ball Run.

At the same time, a young man named Johnny Joestar, a wheelchair user and former rider, manages to watch the start of the race. There he witnesses a brawl between a pickpocket and a foreigner named Gyro Zeppeli, who uses strange rotating steel balls as a weapon. The main character, discouraged by what he saw, accidentally touches them and for the first time in several years of disability, albeit only for a second, he begins to feel his legs.

Wanting to learn the secret of the steel balls, Johnny Joestar meets Gyro and becomes a participant in the race, not even imagining what adventure awaits him.

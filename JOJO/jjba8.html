Description:

After a sudden earthquake on the outskirts of the Japanese city of Morioh, a girl named Hirose Yasuho finds a strange naked guy, half buried underground. He doesn't remember anything: not his name, not where he's from. All he has is a sailor cap, a birthmark in the shape of a five-pointed star, and one of the most incredible Stands.

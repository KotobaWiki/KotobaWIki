Description:

"Stone Ocean" tells the story of Jolyne Kujo, the daughter of Jotaro Kujo, imprisoned in Green Dolphin Street Prison for a crime she did not commit. But instead of the boring life of a prisoner, Jolyne is in for an incredible adventure full of brutal confrontations and stunning revelations, because somewhere inside this strange prison is hiding the one who is to blame for her sentence. Why is all this? What does Jolyne's father have to do with it? What did the mysterious organizer of this madness want and did Dio even have a hand in it?
